FAQ

What are the time restrictions of buying a ticket online?
Online booking is available for all flights up to 2 hours before scheduled departure time. Online booking is available for any flight, with a scheduled departure up to 361 days in the future.
What is the maximum number of travellers for whom I can make an online reservation?
In one online transaction, you can book up to nine (9) passengers, including adults andchildren in your traveling party. Infants are co-related to the number of adults in the travelingparty. The figure could vary in Business and First class depending on the configuration of theaircraft.
Can I make an online booking for a child traveling alone?
No, you cannot make a booking for a child travelling alone. Please contact your nearest Air India office for further details.
How can I book a one way or return travel?
Select the "One way” radio button on the "Search Flights” tab for one-way travel. The "Return” radio button is used for booking return point to point travel.
How can I book a flight that returns to/from a different airport?
Please use the "Multi-City” link to enter details of your separate flights.
